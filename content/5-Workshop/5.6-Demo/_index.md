This demo section shows how the HaShop system works after being deployed on AWS. The content focuses on two main perspectives: the customer shopping flow and the administrator management flow.

From the customer side, the demo covers browsing products, searching/filtering products, viewing product details, adding items to the cart, checking out, receiving notification emails, and confirming delivery. From the admin side, the demo shows user management, category management, product management, order management, and delivery status updates.
